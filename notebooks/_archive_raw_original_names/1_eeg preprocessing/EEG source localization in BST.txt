***In EEG source localization, "BEM" stands for "Boundary Element Method," which is a mathematical technique used to model the electrical conductivity of the head to accurately locate the source of brain activity recorded by EEG electrodes, providing a more realistic representation compared to simpler spherical models; essentially, it helps pinpoint the origin of brain signals by considering the complex shapes of the skull and scalp layers***
